The Plan for the Application

I am building a simple application which fetches and displays the most recent news pertaining generally to technology and gaming from multiple sources (with one source being the New York Times, and another being steampowered.com's open news api). The application is intended to be used as a home page for a browser.

This week's additions:

KNOWN ISSUE:
- Resizing the window on Google Chrome causes the auto-resizing to act strangely. This is due to the images and carousel causing bootstrap's resize mechanism to not function properly on certain browsers, and is not an easy fix. The issue is not present on pages without these elements, such as the search page.
